The Problem

Every Missed Call Is a Lost Customer

Restaurants lose thousands in revenue every month to unanswered phone calls. Here's what's really happening:

Calls Go Unanswered

During busy service, no one picks up. That's 5-15 lost bookings per day — each worth £50-200 in revenue.

Staff Cost a Fortune

A full-time receptionist costs £22,000-28,000/year. Most restaurants can't justify it, so the phone sits there ringing.

Closed Hours = Zero Bookings

60% of booking calls happen outside operating hours. Voicemail doesn't book tables — it loses customers.

Customers Don't Wait

If you don't answer in 3 rings, they call the next restaurant. You never know what you lost.

Booking Errors Pile Up

Wrong dates, wrong times, missed dietary notes. Handwritten bookings taken mid-rush lead to no-shows and complaints.

Staff Are Stretched Thin

Your team is cooking, serving, and managing. Answering the phone pulls them away from the guests who are already there.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it really sound human?
Yes. We use the latest AI voice technology with a natural British accent. Your guests won't know they're speaking to an AI unless you tell them. We'll let you hear it on your demo call.
How does it connect to my booking diary?
We integrate directly with Google Calendar, and we're adding support for OpenTable, ResDiary, and other platforms. Your AI receptionist checks live availability and creates real bookings — no double-entry needed.
What happens if the AI can't handle a call?
If a call requires a human — a complex complaint, a private event enquiry, or something unusual — the AI takes the caller's details and sends you an instant alert so you can call them back. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Can I customise how the AI speaks and what it says?
Absolutely. You choose the name, voice, tone, and personality. We train the AI on your specific menu, policies, opening hours, and any special instructions. It represents your restaurant, not ours.
How long does setup take?
Most restaurants are live within 48 hours. We handle everything — you just fill in a short onboarding form with your restaurant details, and we do the rest.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No long-term contracts. Pay monthly, cancel anytime. We believe you'll stay because the results speak for themselves, not because you're locked in.
